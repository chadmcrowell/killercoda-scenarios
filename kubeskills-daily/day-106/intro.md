# Pod Disruption Budget Blocking Node Drain

Welcome to this lab on pod disruption budget misconfigurations. Pod disruption budgets are designed to protect application availability during voluntary disruptions like node drains, but when the budget is too strict no drain can ever complete. In this lab you will find the blocking PDB, understand the configuration error, and resolve it so cluster maintenance can proceed.
