### What You Learned

Endpoint slices are automatically generated by the endpointslice controller based on Service selector label matching
A Service with zero endpoints will accept connections but immediately reset them causing cryptic timeout errors
The relationship between Service selectors and pod labels must be an exact match including key casing
Headless services behave differently from ClusterIP services when endpoints are missing
Kube-proxy programs iptables rules only for endpoints that exist so missing endpoints mean no routing rules

### Why It Matters

Services with empty endpoint slices are a leading cause of application outages that are initially misdiagnosed as application crashes or network policies. In production this pattern often occurs after a deployment label change or a botched rollout where the selector and pod template labels fall out of sync.
