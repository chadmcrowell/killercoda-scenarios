# Endpoint Slices - When Services Have No Backends

In this lab you will diagnose a Service that appears healthy but has no active endpoints, causing all traffic to fail silently. You will learn how to inspect endpoint slices, understand label selector matching, and trace how a small label discrepancy can take down an entire service. This is a core debugging skill tested on the CKAD and CKA exams.
