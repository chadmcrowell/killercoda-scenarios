# PersistentVolume Reclaim Policy - Data Loss on PVC Delete

Welcome to the PV reclaim policy lab. A developer on your team deleted what they thought was a temporary PVC only to discover it held important application data that is now gone. Your job is to understand exactly why this happened, learn to recognize dangerous storage configurations, and implement reclaim policies that protect data in production environments.
