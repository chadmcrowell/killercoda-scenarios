# Service Account Least Privilege Audit and Remediation

Welcome to the Service Account Permissions lab. In this scenario several applications are running with service accounts that have been granted excessive permissions. Your job is to audit those permissions, understand what each workload actually needs, and then replace the broad role bindings with narrowly scoped alternatives that follow least privilege principles.
