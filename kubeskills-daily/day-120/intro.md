# CPU Throttling - The Silent Performance Killer

CPU limits in Kubernetes are enforced at the kernel level and can silently throttle your application without any pod restarts or error logs. In this lab you will learn to identify CPU throttling through metrics and resource inspection, understand why it happens, and tune your resource limits to eliminate the performance bottleneck.
