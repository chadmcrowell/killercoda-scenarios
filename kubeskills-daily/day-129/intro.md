# Sidecar and Init Container Failures - Multi-Container Pod Debugging

Welcome to a debugging challenge involving multi-container pods. You have three pods that are all failing but for completely different reasons related to their init containers and sidecars. Your job is to use the tools available to you to look inside each pod at the container level, read the right logs, understand what each container needs to run, and fix the configuration issues causing the failures.
