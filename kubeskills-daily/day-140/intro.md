# Deployment Rollout Stuck - Progressive Delivery Failures

Welcome to the stuck rollout lab. A deployment update was triggered twenty minutes ago and it has not completed. The team reports that some users are seeing errors while others are not, which is a classic sign of a mixed-version state. Your mission is to determine why the rollout froze, assess the impact, and get the deployment back to a consistent healthy state.
