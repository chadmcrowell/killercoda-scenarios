# etcd Performance Degradation and Control Plane Slowdown

Welcome to this lab on etcd performance degradation. You will investigate why the Kubernetes control plane is responding slowly, trace the problem to etcd health and maintenance issues, and restore normal performance. etcd health is foundational to cluster stability and understanding its failure modes is critical for platform engineers.
