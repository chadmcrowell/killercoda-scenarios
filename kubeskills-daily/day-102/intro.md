# Graceful Pod Shutdown and Connection Draining

Welcome to this lab on graceful pod shutdown configuration. You will investigate why rolling updates cause connection errors, understand the sequence of events during pod termination, and configure preStop hooks and termination settings to achieve zero-downtime deployments. This is one of the most impactful quality-of-life improvements you can make to any web service running on Kubernetes.
