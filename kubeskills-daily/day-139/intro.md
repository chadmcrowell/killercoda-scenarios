# Pod Scheduling Failures - NodeSelector and Resource Conflicts

Welcome to the pod scheduling failure lab. A critical application pod has been stuck in Pending state for several minutes and the development team is alarmed. Your job is to investigate why the Kubernetes scheduler cannot place this pod and restore normal scheduling behavior. You will use event inspection and node label analysis to find the root cause.
