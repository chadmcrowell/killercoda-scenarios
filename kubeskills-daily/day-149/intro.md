# Network Policy Egress Controls - Locking Down Outbound Pod Traffic

Welcome to the Network Policy Egress lab. The workloads in this cluster have unrestricted outbound access meaning any pod can reach any destination on the internet or within the cluster. Your job is to audit what connections are actually needed, write egress network policies that permit only legitimate outbound traffic, and verify that unauthorized egress is blocked.
