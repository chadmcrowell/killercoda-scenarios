# Persistent Volume Reclaim Policy - The Deleted Volume Problem

In this lab you will explore the consequences of different PersistentVolume reclaim policies by working with Released, Retained, and deleted volumes. You will learn how to recover data from a Released volume by rebinding it to a new PVC, understand why the Delete policy requires careful consideration before use, and configure StorageClass defaults to match organizational data retention requirements.
