# RBAC Misconfiguration - Privilege Escalation Through Overly Permissive Roles

A junior developer service account in the dev namespace has been reported as having unexpected access to secrets and deployments in other namespaces. A security audit flagged this account as a potential privilege escalation risk. Your job is to investigate the RBAC configuration, understand how the overly broad access was granted, and fix it.
