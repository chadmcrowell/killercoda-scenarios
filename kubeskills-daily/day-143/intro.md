# Kubernetes Certificate Rotation - Expiration Without Warning

Welcome to the certificate rotation lab. An alert has fired indicating that cluster certificates will expire within 30 days and the team has never performed a certificate rotation on this cluster before. Your job is to understand the current certificate state, execute a renewal, and verify the cluster continues to function normally. Doing this in advance is the only acceptable strategy.
