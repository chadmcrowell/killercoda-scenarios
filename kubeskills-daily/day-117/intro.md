# Cluster Autoscaler Node Addition With Pending Pods

Welcome to the Cluster Autoscaler lab. Your team enabled the cluster autoscaler last week and it has been faithfully adding nodes in response to demand. However your monitoring dashboard shows dozens of pods still stuck in pending state even as the node count grows. The cloud bill is climbing but availability is not improving. Your task is to figure out why the new nodes are not accepting the pending pods and fix the underlying configuration mismatch.
