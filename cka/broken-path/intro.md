### Welcome!

A team reports their application is unreachable from inside the cluster. The pods show `Running` and the Service exists — so why can't clients connect?

In this scenario, you'll **trace the network path** from a test pod through the Service to the container, **find the port misconfiguration** that breaks it, and **fix** the wiring so the app is reachable.
