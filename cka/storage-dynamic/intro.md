### Welcome!

In this scenario, you'll work with Kubernetes **StorageClasses** and **PersistentVolumeClaims** to understand how dynamic provisioning works — and how reclaim policies affect what happens to your data after a PVC is deleted.

Your cluster has a default `local-path` StorageClass. You'll create a new StorageClass with a `Retain` policy, promote it as the new default, and deploy a workload that uses it.
